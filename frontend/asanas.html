Yoga Asanas

Downward Facing Dog

Downward Facing Dog, or Adho Mukha Svanasana in Sanskrit, is a foundational yoga pose that stretches the entire body, particularly the back, hamstrings, calves, and shoulders.

Tree Pose

Tree Pose, or Vrksasana in Sanskrit, is a standing balance pose that strengthens the legs, improves concentration, and promotes stability and balance.

Warrior II

Warrior II, or Virabhadrasana II in Sanskrit, is a standing yoga pose that strengthens the legs, opens the hips and chest, and improves concentration and focus.

Child's Pose

Child's Pose, or Balasana in Sanskrit, is a resting yoga pose that stretches the spine, hips, and thighs while promoting relaxation and stress relief.
